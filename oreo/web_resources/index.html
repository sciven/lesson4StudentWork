Sparc Project Oreo!

My Sparc device is a super cool little device I can use to track things.

Why? Why not its got buttons! Plus I get to try programing and playing with an Arduino.

About My Device

I used an Arduino Flora to make my tracking device,
I made a device that can track actions with the simple click of a button.

I chose to track personal health which has 3 options (1 for each button) the options include, drinking a glass of water, exercising fo 30 minutes and Eating a serving of fruit.
