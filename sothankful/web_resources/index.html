Instructions on downloading the sparc connector and uploading to the web

Download the sparc connector

  1. go to Sparc Download Page
  2. save the zip file to a location where you will be able to extract it. We recommend your desktop
  3. after you have extracted the file, open the file named SparcConnectorApp

Set your Arduino device to sync with an initiative

  1. to see the existing initiatives associated with the arduino device, press load from arduino
  2. to set a new initiative, press CANCEL and then check the ports you have.
  3. Set the initiative from the left hand side of the screen
  4. another step and then use your device???

use your device

  1. make sure you have turned turned on both on buttons - one on the battery pack and one on the arduino device
  2. Use your arduino device and click your buttons :)

Upload to the web

  1. connect your adrunio via a usb cable to your computer
  2. open the Sparc Connector app as mentioned above
  3. click on upload to web
